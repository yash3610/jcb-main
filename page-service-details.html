FAQ

Here are common questions customers ask before booking AC service or repair.

  • How often should an AC be serviced?
    For regular home use, service before summer and once after heavy usage is ideal. Offices and shops may need quarterly cleaning.
  • Do you check gas before refilling?
    Yes. We first check pressure and possible leakage. Gas charging without leak inspection can lead to repeated cooling failure.
  • Can you fix water leakage?
    Most leakage issues come from blocked drains, dirty coils or incorrect slope. We inspect and correct the exact cause.
  • Do you provide AMC visits?
    Yes, AMC plans are available for homes, offices, clinics and shops that need scheduled maintenance reminders.
