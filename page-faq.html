  • Why is my AC running but not cooling?
    Weak cooling can happen due to dirty coils, blocked filters, low refrigerant, outdoor unit heat buildup or a faulty capacitor. A technician check confirms the cause.
  • How often should I service my AC?
    For home use, service at least twice a year. Heavy-use office or commercial AC units should be cleaned every three to four months.
  • Do you repair water leakage from indoor units?
    Yes. We clean the drain line, inspect the drain tray, check unit slope and clear coil dirt that can cause water dripping.
  • Can I book AMC for multiple AC units?
    Yes, AMC plans can be customized for homes, offices, clinics, shops and commercial locations with multiple AC units.
