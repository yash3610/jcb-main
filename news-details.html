5 signs your AC needs servicing soon

If your AC takes longer than usual to cool the room, the first thing to check is airflow. Dirty filters, dust-filled coils and a blocked blower can reduce cooling even when the compressor is working.

Water leakage, ice formation, unusual vibration and bad smell are also early warnings. These issues are easier and cheaper to fix when they are handled before the machine stops completely.

A proper service visit should include filter washing, coil cleaning, drain flushing, outdoor unit inspection, electrical checks and final temperature testing. That is how you keep comfort steady through summer.
