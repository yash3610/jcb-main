Home split AC installation with clean routing

The customer needed a new split AC installed in a bedroom with minimum visible piping and safe outdoor unit placement. Our technician inspected the wall strength, drain slope and electrical point before beginning the installation.

The work included indoor unit mounting, copper pipe connection, insulation, outdoor stand fixing, vacuuming, leakage check and final cooling trial. The customer received usage and filter-cleaning guidance before handover.

Project challenges

The outdoor space was narrow and the drain line needed careful routing to prevent future leakage. We adjusted the pipe path without disturbing the room finish.

Result delivered

The unit reached stable cooling during the trial run, noise levels were normal and drainage worked correctly. The installation was completed in a single visit.
